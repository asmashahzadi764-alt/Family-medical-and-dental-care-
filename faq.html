Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions our Multan patients ask us most often. Still have one? Reach out to us directly.

Do you really try to save my natural teeth instead of extracting them? expand_more

Yes — it's the core of Dr. Arif Siddiqui's approach. We exhaust conservative, restorative options like root canals and fillings before ever recommending an extraction.

What are your clinic hours? expand_more

We're open Monday to Saturday, 10:00 AM–2:00 PM and 5:00 PM–9:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays.

How do I book an appointment? expand_more

Use the "Book Appointment" button at the top of any page, or visit our booking page directly. Choose a service, date, and time slot, and our team will confirm with you.

Do you treat children? expand_more

Yes, we welcome patients of all ages, including pediatric dental care for children.

Do you offer both medical and dental consultations? expand_more

Yes — Family Medical And Dental Care combines general medical consultations with advanced dentistry under one roof at Loha Market Rd, Multan.

Where are you located? expand_more

We're on Loha Market Rd, Kiri Dawood Khan, Multan, Punjab, Pakistan. See the map on our Contact page for directions.
