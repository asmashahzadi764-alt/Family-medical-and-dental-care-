Privacy Policy

Family Medical And Dental Care ("we", "us", "our clinic") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

What we collect

When you book an appointment or send us an inquiry through this website, we collect the information you provide directly: your full name, phone number, the service you're interested in, your preferred appointment date and time, and any message you write to us.

How we use it

This information is used only to schedule and manage your appointment, respond to your inquiry, and contact you about your visit to our clinic in Multan. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How we store it

Appointment and inquiry details are stored securely and are only accessible to authorized clinic staff for the purpose of managing your care.

Your rights

You can ask us to review, correct, or delete the information we hold about you at any time by contacting the clinic directly using the details below.
